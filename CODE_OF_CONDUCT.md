# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), summarised below for clarity.

## Standards

We expect everyone interacting with this project — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other community space — to:

- Be respectful and welcoming. Assume good intent.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Take responsibility for mistakes, learn, and move on.

The following behaviours are not welcome:

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment.
- Discriminatory language or imagery.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussion.
- Other conduct a reasonable person would consider unprofessional.

## Scope

This applies inside all project spaces (this repository, its issue tracker, pull requests, and any official communication channels) and when an individual is officially representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions, and temporarily or permanently ban contributors whose behaviour they consider inappropriate.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behaviour by opening a private email to the maintainers. The maintainer email is project-specific and listed in the repository's `package.json` under `bugs.email` or in the GitHub repository contact metadata; if neither is set, please open a minimal public issue asking for a private contact and a maintainer will follow up.

All reports are handled confidentially. We will respond as quickly as we can.
